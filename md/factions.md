ORION FACTION
    There's a reason everyone gets their start in Orion. It just so turns out that space travel is hard! And getting started is even harder. The technology required as a base for space travel just isn't something someone can assemble or gather by themselves. But nonetheless, space needs to be explored! Space needs to be something materially gatherable for the sapient forces of this world. And in a greedy world, where nobody found it their responsibility, someone had to step up. Thus the Orion Faction - They'll get you started. Such a responsibility comes at a high cost. Loyalty. Those who believe in their mission - which is to explore, defend, make use of, and persist space and sapience - do so with vigor by oath. In Orion Space, crime is not tolerated, and is dealt with in a handly manner by quick-response enforcers who respond to distress calls and handle the situation accordingly.

DRYADS FACTION
    Summary: The Dryads are a decentralized faction of digitized minds controlling self-replicating nanites and machines that spread like and mimic a growing ecosystem.

    Aesthetic:
    The Dryads are a "Gray Goo" kind of faction that continually terraforms/corrupts the environments they inhabit. Primarily, they create metal forests of trees with solar panel leaves, tin foil thin metal grass and flowers, etc., Alongside mechanical wildlife. They create an artifical mimicry of nature that slowly spreads outwards.

    Goals:
    The main goal of the Dryads is to live forever. They have left their bodies behind, but are looking to perfect their digital existence and spread to such an extent that they can never be eradicated, can exist until the end of the universe, and can perhaps even explore (if only in the aggregate as a faction) the whole universe. To this end they are focused on spreading and self-preservation. 

    Niche:
    The Dryads, being inorganic digital creatures controlling mechanical automata proxies, focus on colonizing and extracting resources from uninhabitable worlds. They grow like lichen on barren worlds rich in metals, but will also inhabit conventionally habitable worlds. They will trade raw metals, silicates and advanced electronics to other factions, buying organic materials that are not abundant on the hostile worlds they inhabit, especially things like rubber or other oil products. 

    History:
    The Dryads came originally from a transhumanist post-mortal ideological group who saw the biological body as the main limitation to the progression and continued existence of intelligent life in the universe. By digitizing their minds they permit themselves to colonize uninhabitable worlds, travel massive relativistic distances without issue, and they hope to escape death in all its forms and exist until all the stars in the universe burn out.

    Details I think are important:
    Dryads are anarchists, acting more like a general force or movement of people choosing to abandon the flesh.
    Dryads are called Dryads because their minds exist on physical servers enclosed in datacenters, like a woodland spirit tied to a specific tree. These datacenters also tend to be buried underground beneath their signature metal forests.
    Dryads inhabit automata proxies to interact with other sapient people, like ghosts possessing animals or empty shells.
